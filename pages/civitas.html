CIVITAS

A educational institution based on platonic values. The aim is to inform a ruling class of youth through meritocratic admission and hollistic development. Students admitted between the ages of 10 and 17.

CURRICULUM

Students will have a curriculum that emphasizes curiousity and the desire to seek knowledge above all. In the humanities and sciences individual study will be a core focus. Language learning, physical education, and mathematics will be mandatory. All else will be semi-optional. Language emphasis will be on latin and mandarin, though other languages will be encouraged. Physical education will focus on sports such as basketball, football, soccer, martial arts, and capture the flag. Minimum mathematics achievement will be a completion of calculus. There will be no grades but required test scores on final exams. There will also be weekend leadership retreats and community service projects. Assignments for classes such as history, literature, and science will be custom designed to challenge and engage students at a per student focus.
